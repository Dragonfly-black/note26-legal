Privacy Policy for Note26

Last updated: November 2025

Thank you for using Note26 (“the App”).
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our services.

By using Note26, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

⸻

1. Information We Collect

1.1 User-Provided Data

We collect the following information when you choose to provide it:
	•	Notes and reminders created in the app
	•	Calendar entries or daily planning data
	•	Optional metadata (timestamps, device info for debugging)

1.2 Authentication Data

If you sign in using Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In, we receive:
	•	Email address (may be private email relay from Apple)
	•	User name (optional)
	•	Authentication identifiers
This information is used only to create and maintain your account.

1.3 Automatically Collected Data

We may collect:
	•	Device type and OS version
	•	App usage analytics (non-identifying)
	•	Crash logs
This data is anonymous and used to improve app stability.

⸻

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
	•	Sync notes and reminders across devices
	•	Provide account login and data backup
	•	Improve app performance and stability
	•	Diagnose crashes or bugs
	•	Deliver subscription-related features (Starter / Plus plans)

We do not sell or share your information with third-party advertisers.

⸻

3. Data Storage (Firebase / Firestore)

Note26 uses Firebase services provided by Google, including:
	•	Cloud Firestore (for storing notes, reminders, and settings)
	•	Firebase Authentication (for login)

Your data may be processed or stored on Google servers located outside your country of residence.

All communication is encrypted in transit and at rest.

⸻

4. User Ownership of Content

All notes, reminders, and personal content you create remain your property.
We access this data only to deliver app features such as sync and backup.

You may request permanent deletion of your data by contacting us.

⸻

5. Subscription Information

Note26 offers auto-renewing monthly subscriptions:
	•	Starter ($2.99/month)
	•	Plus ($3.99/month)

5.1 Auto-Renewal

Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

5.2 Payment

Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at the time of purchase.

5.3 Manage or Cancel

You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime in your Apple ID settings.

5.4 Refunds

Refund requests must be handled through Apple, as we do not process refunds directly.

⸻

6. Children’s Privacy

Note26 is not intended for children under 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

⸻

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
Continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

⸻

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or data deletion requests:
